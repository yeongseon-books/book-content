# What is an AI Agent?

> AI Agent 101 Series (1/10)

If you've used ChatGPT, you're familiar with how models answer questions. But AI agents take it a step further. They don't just respond—they call tools, verify results, and decide what to do next. This difference may seem small, but it transforms LLMs from simple conversation partners into automated task executors.

The essence of an agent is the **observe → think → act → check** loop. It observes the situation, decides what to do (think), executes tools (act), and verifies the results (check). This loop repeats until the goal is achieved. ChatGPT answers once and stops, but agents act multiple times pursuing a goal.

This article covers the core concept of AI agents. We'll explore the difference between ChatGPT and agents, agent components, real-world use cases, and when agents are necessary versus when they're not.
